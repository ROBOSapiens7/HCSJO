    Programs and Health Services

    Find clinical programs and services available at Mount Sinai St. Luke’s.

    • AIDS/HIV Center

      AIDS/HIV Center

      HIV specialists at the Center for Comprehensive Care are ready to help patients receive holistic care by offering a complete range of services including dental care, behavioral health, and an on-site pharmacy.

    • Addiction Institute of New York

      Addiction Institute of New York

      The Addiction Institute of New York provides state-of-the-art, evidence-based treatment, training of professionals in addiction medicine, and research on the treatment of addictive disorders.

    • Al-Sabah Arrhythmia Institute

      Al-Sabah Arrhythmia Institute

      Expert cardiologists at the Al-Sabah Arrhythmia Institute utilize various diagnostic tests and offer a full spectrum of non-surgical and surgical approaches for the treatment of all arrhythmia conditions.

    • Breast Surgery

      Breast Surgery

      Our breast surgeons are proud to work with outstanding plastics surgeons, radiologists, pathologists, medical and radiation oncologists, as well as physician assistants, nurses, and others in caring for our patients. The overall team here at Mount Sinai St. Luke’s Roosevelt is extremely strong both clinically and in clinical research.

    • Blood Donation Services

      Blood Donation Services

      Donating blood is an easy way you can help make a difference in your community without going through great expense or taking a lot of time. Your donation can help save lives!

    • Bone Density Testing

      Bone Density Testing

      Osteoporosis is a serious and common disease that can be present without your knowledge for decades because osteoporosis doesn't cause symptoms until a bone fractures. Bone density testing is a simple screening recommended for those who are most likely to develop osteoporosis.

    • Cardiology

      Cardiology

      With an expert team of cardiologists, interventional cardiologists, and cardiac surgeons, our cardiac program stands at the forefront of state-of-the-art diagnostics and curative measures that can help stop heart disease in its tracks.

    • Comprehensive Breast Center

      Comprehensive Breast Center

      The Breast Surgery Service is both clinically and academically active in the fight against breast cancer. Patients are kept informed through weekly Breast Conferences that review the latest developments and protocols.

    • Cancer Center

      Cancer Center

      The Cancer Center offers the best possible outcomes by delivering high-quality, comprehensive medical care while maintaining patients' overall quality of life.

    • Cardiac Catheterization Lab

      Cardiac Catheterization Lab

      Interventional cardiologists at the Cardiac Catheterization Lab use minimally invasive, non-surgical procedures to treat cardiovascular disease, attending to each patient's needs with the highest levels of proficiency and compassion.

    • Cardiovascular Disease Prevention Program

      Cardiovascular Disease Prevention Program

      The Cardiovascular Disease Prevention Program is dedicated to improving the lives of patients with heart and vascular disease, and identifying and reducing risk for those who could be afflicted. The program is ideally suited to helping diabetic patients as well.

    • Child and Family Institute

      Child and Family Institute

      We are dedicated to improving the mental health of every child and adolescent within our community, while providing support for parents, caretakers and other family members.

    • Child Life Program

      Child Life Program

      Specialists at the Child Life Program help ease your child's anxiety during hospitalization by creating a supportive environment to meet your child's developmental, emotional, and psychological needs.

    • Coronary Artery Calcium Detection Program

      Coronary Artery Calcium Detection Program

      The Coronary Artery Calcium Detection Program offers coronary artery calcium scans to detect the presence and extent of coronary artery disease or your risk for developing heart disease.

    • Crime Victims Treatment Center

      Crime Victims Treatment Center

      With over thirty years of experience, the Crimes Victims Treatment Center is the largest, most comprehensive hospital-based victim assistance program in New York State. The Center offers various compassionate clinical and supportive services to help empower survivors of violence.

    • Dermatology

      Dermatology

      We believe everyone deserves to have healthy skin. That’s why our extraordinary team of experts is dedicated to understanding and treating a diverse range of skin conditions in patients of all ages and skin types.

    • Emergency Department

      Emergency Department

      The Emergency Department is staffed by doctors who are Board Certified in Emergency Medicine as well as by doctors in every clinical specialty that are available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

    • Endocrinology and Diabetes

      Endocrinology and Diabetes

    • Endovascular Surgery

      Endovascular Surgery

      Our specially trained doctors treat a variety of disorders without open surgery by using x-rays and other imaging techniques to guide small tubes and devices through blood vessels. These minimally invasive techniques treat disorders of the brain, head, neck, and spine.

    • Geriatrics

      Geriatrics

      The Division of Geriatrics is dedicated to maintaining the well-being and vitality of older adults. Our goal is to help the aging live up to their full potential.We interact with caregivers and family members on a personal level, as well as a clinical level, as we all work together to provide a consistently high level of patient care.

    • Hand Surgery Center

      Hand Surgery Center

      The Hand Surgery Center has cared for hand, wrist, elbow, and shoulder disorders in patients of all ages for over fifty years. Continuative research and Certificates of Added Qualification in Hand Surgery make our surgeons familiar with various hand conditions and possible treatments.

    • The Headache Institute

      The Headache Institute

      The Headache Institute is dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of headaches by offering patients the possibility of hope and relief. Through cutting-edge research and treatments, certified neurologists are responsible for successfully treating never-before-seen conditions.

    • Heart Failure Program

      Heart Failure Program

      The Heart Failure Program aims to improve patients' overall quality of life and reduce risk of death. Rather then focusing on end-stage patients only, the program also focuses on those with earlier stages of the disease to increase long-term outcomes.

    • Heart Surgery

      Heart Surgery

      Cardiac surgery at St. Luke’s is a leader in research, state of the art technology, and compassionate care.

    • Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy Center

      Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy Center

      The Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy Center assists patients with complex cardiac disease symptoms through comprehensive diagnostic evaluations, a wide range of treatments, and screening for relatives of affected patients.

    • Institute for Neurology and Neurosurgery

      Institute for Neurology and Neurosurgery

      The Institute for Neurology and Neurosurgery is a comprehensive, multidisciplinary center for the diagnosis and treatment of adults and children afflicted with neurological disorders. Its humanistic health care attracts patients from all parts of the world.

    • Interventional Cardiology

      Interventional Cardiology

      Our interventional cardiologists use minimally invasive, non-surgical procedures to treat cardiovascular disease. Team members are present at every step to attend to each patient's needs with proficiency and compassion.

    • LGBT Health Services

      LGBT Health Services

      LGBT Health Services is committed to meeting the special health care needs of the lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender (LGBT) community with respect and compassion.

    • Lung Cancer

      Lung Cancer

      Our specialists are on the cutting edge of developments in the diagnosis and treatment of lung and other cancers of the chest region.

    • Mount Sinai Crystal Run Partnership

      Mount Sinai Crystal Run Partnership

      The Mount Sinai-Crystal Run Alliance for Healthcare Transformation is a partnership designed to benefit the health of patients of the Mount Sinai Health System and Crystal Run Healthcare.

    • Neurology

      Neurology

      The highly integrated, multidisciplinary Department of Neurology utilizes advanced diagnostics and recent clinical innovations to evaluate and treat patients with complex neurological disorders.

    • Neurosurgery

      Neurosurgery

      Highly experienced neurosurgeons at the Department of Neurosurgery use cutting-edge technologies to provide comprehensive surgical care to patients suffering from complex pathologies of the brain, spinal cord, and spine.

    • Obstetrics and Gynecology

      Obstetrics and Gynecology

      Obstetricians and gynecologists at the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology are dedicated to offering the finest, most comprehensive women's reproductive health and prenatal care, and are committed to researching the most promising therapies in the field.

    • Orthopedics and Sports Medicine

      Orthopedics and Sports Medicine

      Highly specialized surgeons at the Department of Orthopedic Surgery and Sports Medicine offer a full array of treatments for all muscle, bone, and joint ailments by utilizing the latest techniques and state-of-the-art technologies.

    • Orthopaedic Surgery

      Orthopaedic Surgery

      Highly specialized surgeons at the Department of Orthopedic Surgery and Sports Medicine offer a full array of treatments for all muscle, bone, and joint ailments by utilizing the latest techniques and state-of-the-art technologies.

    • Osteoporosis

      Osteoporosis

      Osteoporosis is a serious and common disease that can be present without your knowledge for decades because osteoporosis doesn't cause symptoms until a bone fractures. Bone density testing is a simple screening recommended for those who are most likely to develop osteoporosis.

    • Pain Management

      Pain Management

      The Manhattan Center for Pain Management has improved the lives of thousands with acute and chronic pain. Led by a board-certified anesthesiologist specializing in pain management, the Center employs a multidisciplinary approach to diagnose and treat a broad range of problems.

    • Pediatric Surgery

      Pediatric Surgery

      Our service provides highly expert, experienced pediatric surgical care at both the Roosevelt and Mount Sinai St. Luke’s campuses. More than 250 pediatric surgical operations are performed annually for wide ranging conditions affecting children in the neonatal and pediatric age groups.

    • Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation

      Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation

      With access to the latest technologies and over 20 years of experience, our Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation specialists restore functional living to patients with a wide range of illnesses and injuries.

    • Plastic/Reconstructive Surgery

      Plastic/Reconstructive Surgery

      Our surgeons specialize in both elective cosmetic surgery as well as complex reconstructive procedures. We have an especially close working relationship with our world class Breast Surgery Service, providing the most sophisticated techniques in breast reconstruction.

    • Primary Care

      Primary Care

      Mount Sinai St. Luke’s offers many primary care locations with extended hours and convenient locations.

    • Psychiatry

      Psychiatry

      The Department of Psychiatry empowers individuals to change and improve their lives through providing the very best in behavioral health services as well as consumer education, outreach, and community collaboration.

    • Pulmonary, Critical Care and Sleep Medicine

      Pulmonary, Critical Care and Sleep Medicine

      The Division of Pulmonary, Critical Care and Sleep Medicine provides diagnostic and treatment services to combat respiratory failure, lung diseases, and sleep-related breathing disorders though all stages of illness.

    • Radiology

      Radiology

      The Radiology Department provides a wide range of outpatient and inpatient diagnostic imaging and interventional services that fully integrate with the services provided by the clinical departments.

    • Radiation Oncology

      Radiation Oncology

      The multidisciplinary Department of Radiation Oncology provides patients with the comprehensive, individualized attention they need, maximizing cures while maintaining optimal organ function and quality of life.

    • Skin of Color (Dermatology)

      Skin of Color (Dermatology)

      The Skin of Color Center addresses the unique needs of patients with pigmented skin, hair, and nails. Board-certified dermatologists specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions that are common or of particular concern in individuals of color through individualized, culturally sensitive care.

    • Stroke Center

      Stroke Center

      The Stroke Center provides rapid diagnoses, expert treatments, and rehabilitation for stroke victims, and helps individuals recognize, reduce, or even eliminate certain risk factors.

    • Surgery Department

      Surgery Department

      The Department of Surgery is widely respected as a sophisticated clinical and academic environment in which physicians practice superior care with compassion and sensitivity and carry out innovative research.

    • Surgical Oncology

      Surgical Oncology

      The Surgical Oncology Division at Mount Sinai St. Luke’s Roosevelt is staffed by a group of experienced cancer surgeons who have trained at the top hospitals and Universities in the United States.

    • The NiteStar Program

      The NiteStar Program

      The NiteStar Program uses a non-threatening approach to provide youth with important health information about the realities of HIV infection and AIDS. The program addresses the difficult decisions and situations faced by young people: clarifying issues, changing attitudes, and promoting healthy behavior.

    • Thoracic Oncology

      Thoracic Oncology

      Our specialists are on the cutting edge of developments in the diagnosis and treatment of lung and other cancers of the chest region.

    • Trauma

      Trauma

      Since 1983, the Trauma Program at Mount Sinai St. Luke’s has been committed to providing optimal care to the patient with traumatic injuries 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

    • University Medical Practice Associates

      University Medical Practice Associates

      The University Medical Practice Associates (UMPA) is just one of our community outreach health services. UMPA offers personalized, integrated treatment with the most advanced techniques and technologies.

    • Urology

      Urology

      The Department of Urology excels at compassionately addressing the emotional and physical concerns of patients with infections or other conditions affecting the urinary and genital tracts in men and women.

    • Vascular Malformations Center

      Vascular Malformations Center

      The Vascular Malformations Center offers patients and families from around the world access to state-of-the art technologies, along with leading physicians who are dedicated to treating vascular diseases throughout the body.

    • Weight Loss Surgery

      Weight Loss Surgery ›

      Bariatric surgeons at the Center for Bariatric Surgery and Metabolic Disease at Mount Sinai St. Luke’s Roosevelt have a long tradition of treating obesity. The program is also a center of excellence and is part of the hospital’s Division of Minimally Invasive Surgery. Certified as an American College of Surgeons Center of Excellence for Obesity Surgery, the Center is also affiliated with the New York Center for Obesity Research at Mount Sinai St. Luke’s, which is nationally recognized for its expertise in the study and treatment of obesity.

    • West Care Medical Associates

      West Care Medical Associates

      A well-established Primary Care Physician Network with eight locations in Manhattan (New York City) designed to meet the needs of today's health care consumers.

    • West Park Medical Group

      West Park Medical Group

      West Park Medical Group (WPMG) is a group practice specializing in primary care for adults (internal medicine) and primary care for children (pediatrics). All WPMG doctors are board certified in their specialty and strive to provide respectful and compassionate care to their patients.

    • Women’s Heart NY

      Women’s Heart NY

      Women’s Heart NY is a multi-site comprehensive heart program designed specifically for women who are at risk for, or have a history of heart disease. Diagnostic tests and treatments are available for a wide spectrum of heart diseases, including coronary artery disease, valvular heart disease, arrhythmias and congestive heart failure.
